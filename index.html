Everyone contributed equally to the debate. In the end, when the judges announce the winner, we were overjoyed! The judges commented that though the B team fought well, it was the A team which had knowledge and fluency on its side!

What's New

    New School Canteen

    The Municipality of Torres Vedras has its own school food program (Sustainable Food School Program(link is external)), characterized by a network strategy which delegates the competences to the 12 local county authorities, which, themselves contract local private social institutions for the management of the school meals. The menus are carefully prepared with the support of a nutritionist, respecting the nutritional balance, taking into account anthropometric values and favouring local seasonal foods, traditional dishes or the most appreciated in each location. Numbers talking, about 2700 meals/day are provided by these social institutions, while 1300 are provided from the two municipal central kitchens.

    Implemented Online Attendance Managment System

    Teachers have instant visibility into their classes attendance for easy oversight during the school day. They will be able to see attendance being recorded in real time as students scan into class or teachers can record attendance themselves. Attendance statuses are automatically sent to the front office and your SIS. If a child doesn’t show up when expected, the parent can receive an automatic alert.
